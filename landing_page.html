FAQ

Common questions

What assets do you cover? +
Starter covers the top 4 crypto pairs (BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB). Pro and Enterprise add US stocks (NVDA, AAPL, TSLA, SPY, QQQ) and major forex pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY). Custom watchlists are available on Enterprise.
How many signals per week? +
Starter receives up to 15 signals per week. Pro receives up to 40. Quality is always prioritised over quantity — signals are filtered to confidence ≥65% and R:R ≥1.5:1, so some weeks may have fewer but higher-quality signals.
How does the AI actually work? +
Six specialised agents run in parallel: a data crawler fetches price data and news, a technical agent computes 20+ indicators, a sentiment agent uses NLP to score news headlines, a pattern agent detects chart patterns, a strategy engine runs a 6-strategy weighted ensemble, and a risk agent computes ATR-based TP/SL levels. Only signals where multiple agents agree are published.
Is this financial advice? +
No. NEXUS AI provides informational trading signals only. We are not regulated financial advisors. Never risk money you cannot afford to lose, and always conduct your own research before making any trading decision. Past signal performance does not guarantee future results.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, subscriptions can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. You retain access until the end of your current billing period. Contact us via Telegram or email and cancellation is processed within 24 hours.
Do you offer an API? +
Yes. Pro and Enterprise subscribers receive an API key to access signals programmatically via REST endpoints. Enterprise also includes webhook delivery with HMAC-signed payloads so signals push directly to your system in real time.
